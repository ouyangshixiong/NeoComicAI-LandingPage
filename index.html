从“生成漫画图”升级为“生产漫剧资产”From image generation to short-drama production

NeonComicAI-Agent 的新方向不是把一次出图包装成创作工具，而是把短剧生产拆成普通用户能理解的对话式工作流：故事输入、剧本改写、镜头拆分、分镜图、故事板、视频分片、覆盖核对和成片导出。 The product is no longer framed as a comic-image generator. It turns short-drama production into an approachable workflow: story input, rewriting, shot breakdown, storyboard images, video segments, coverage review and export.

常见问题FAQ

评估前最应该确认的事What to check before adopting

它和普通 AI 视频生成工具有什么区别？How is it different from a normal AI video generator?

普通工具通常只处理一次生成请求。NeonComicAI-Agent 更像生产中台：它管理故事、IP 资产、分镜、故事板、场景图、视频候选、覆盖核对和导出。Most generators handle a single request. NeonComicAI-Agent manages stories, IP assets, panels, storyboards, scene references, video candidates, coverage review and export.

漫画分镜还重要吗？Do comic-style panels still matter?

重要，但它们是视频生产的中间资产和兼容输出。主交付方向是漫剧 / 短剧视频资产，分镜图负责对齐镜头设计、角色一致性和视频参考。Yes, as intermediate assets and compatible deliverables. The main direction is short-drama video production, where panels align shot design and references.

能否只用部分节点？Can teams use only part of the workflow?

可以。团队可以只用剧本拆分、资产库、分镜图、故事板生成、视频核对或 Agent 自动化中的某几段，把它接入已有制作流程。Yes. Teams can adopt script breakdown, asset libraries, storyboard generation, video review or agent automation independently.

数据和素材如何保留？Where do data and assets live?

项目默认以自托管服务、数据库和素材目录管理。图片、视频、任务状态和生成记录都能在工作台里回看，便于迁移、复核和继续生产。The default model is self-hosted services, database and asset directories. Images, videos, job state and generation records remain reviewable in the workspace.
